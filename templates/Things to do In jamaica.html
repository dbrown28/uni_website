Top 5 Activities To Do In Jamaica

1. White River Tubing in Ocho Rios

White River Tubing is a refreshing adventure in Ocho Rios, Jamaica. The White River is known for its beauty and fresh water rivers. Create some great memories here as you tube down this fresh body of water surrounded by lush and tropical scenery. If you’re looking for a tour that’s a little different, this is perfect for you

2. Glistening Waters (Luminous Lagoon)

Glistening Waters is a magical glowing lagoon on the north coast of Jamaica. This tourist attraction is only found in 4 places in the world, however, this lagoon is the brightest of them all due to consistent climate in jamaica ,Making the lagoon an excellent tourist attratcion in Jamaica.

3. Floyd’s Pelican Bar in St. Elizabeth

Floyd’s Pelican Bar is located in Parottee Bay on the South Coast of Jamaica is one of the most unique places to eat and have a drink in the entire world. It is defiantly something to brag about to friends, not many people can say they've had a drink 3/4 miles out in the sea. One of the best bars in the Caribbean!

4. The Blue Hole in Ocho Rios

Blue hole is a not so common tourist attraction in Jamaica, mainly because it isn’t the easiest attraction to get to. It is located in Ocho Rios. Mystic Mountain Rainforest in Ocho Rios

Mystic Mountain is one of the top attractions in Ocho Rios. On the mystic mountain there are a lot of activities to do from zip lining and bobsledding to bird watching. To get to the mountain you would have to travel up the mountainside via chair lift.
